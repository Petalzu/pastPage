These are my pictures at an ACG exhibition

  • Booth At The ACG Exhibition




    Booth is a place where ACG original works and collaborative content creation works are sold by officials or collaborative content creation enthusiasts. You can buy whatever you want here.
    Many works will also choose to sell in diffuse exhibition to attract popularity. Some of them are handmade by handmakers, which is very unique.
  • Klee



    Klee is one of the characters of the Genshen Impact. She is one of the most popular characters in the game and is very popular at home and abroad. Because of her naughty and lovely characteristics, she is loved by many people.
    There is no doubt that this coser is also one of the most outstanding cosers
  • Cosers On ACG Exhibition



    Cosplay is the abbreviation of COSTUMEPLAY, which refers to playing roles in works by wearing clothes of various roles, including animation, comics and video games. Role playing began in Japan.
    The people who play Cosplay are because they love those people in the second dimension, want to meet their dream of being close to their favorite people.
  • Kirisame Marisa(きりさめ まりさ）



    Marissa is a character in the TOHO PROJECT and one of my favorite characters. You can find many cosers playing different roles in the ACG exhibition.
    You can have a lot of fun in ACG Exhibition,but of course the best souvenir for participating in the ACG exhibition is taking a photo with your favorite Coser. As long as you pay attention to your etiquette.
